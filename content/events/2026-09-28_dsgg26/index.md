We are pleased to participate in the 5th Data Stewardship goes Germany (DSgG) Community Meeting, taking place from **28–29 September 2026** at the University of Cologne under the motto "DSgG – from going to growing".

During the meeting, we will present our poster, "DMP4NFDI Train-the-Trainer Concept: Empowering Data Stewards on Data Management Plans and RDMO". The poster introduces our training concept, which supports data stewards in developing the knowledge and practical skills needed to guide researchers in creating Data Management Plans (DMPs) and using RDMO effectively.

We look forward to exchanging ideas with the community and discussing approaches to strengthening research data management support across institutions.

The DSgG Community Meeting 2026 is organised for the first time by the [Data Stewardship in Germany Community](https://www.forschungsdaten.org/index.php/Kategorie:DSgG). The event will take place in the [Seminar Building](https://maps.uni-koeln.de/?building=106&lang=en) on the central campus and is free of charge.

Further details can be found on the [event website](https://fdm.uni-koeln.de/dsgg26).
