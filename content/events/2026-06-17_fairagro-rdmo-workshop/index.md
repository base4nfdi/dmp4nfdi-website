Join the hands-on workshop **RDMO in Practice: Building Data Management Plans for Agricultural Research on 17–18 June 2026** and gain practical experience creating Data Management Plans for agricultural and agroecosystem research projects. The workshop focuses on the DMP-tool Research Data Management Organizer (RDMO) and the FAIRagro-developed, discipline-specific DMP template. 

The workshop combines thematic introductions with several hands-on sessions structured along the sections of the template. Key components of a DMP — including data types, data documentation, metadata, data publication, and legal aspects — will first be introduced conceptually and then applied directly in RDMO during practical exercises.

Participants are encouraged to bring their own research projects and will have the opportunity to work on their own DMPs throughout the workshop. The interactive format provides ample room for discussion, questions, and exchange between participants and trainers.
