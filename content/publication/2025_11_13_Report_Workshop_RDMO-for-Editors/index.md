abstract: >
  DMP4NFDI held the hands-on online workshop "RDMO for Editors" on 13 and 14 November 2025. The workshop was based on the newly developed Train-the-Trainer concept for DMPs and RDMO, and marked the first test of the Advanced Level of the RDMO module. This level focuses on hands-on usage and familiarisation with the RDMO interface from an editor’s perspective as well as working with the NFDI DMP Template Framework.
